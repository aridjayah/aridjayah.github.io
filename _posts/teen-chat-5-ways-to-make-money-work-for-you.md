2. Take Control
You need to know that now YOU are at the steering wheel of your life. You create every moment in your life, especially your financial life. If you don't believe this, then you may believe that you have little or no control over your life and that financial success has nothing to do with you. You are only putting yourself in the role of a victim and there is no such thing as a rich victim!
